OUR STORY

Born out of wanderlust, Tulua is a brand that speaks of both exuberance and minimalism with its effortless outfits.

Aakanksha, the founder/creator of Tulua, are known to bring out fun and playful personalities with luxurious yet vibrant sensibilities.

Coveted creations illustrate a pure reflection of cosy and vibrant western wear instilling the high spirit of travel in our daily routines and experimenting with slow fashion.
